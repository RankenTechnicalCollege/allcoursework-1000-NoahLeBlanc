Fall 2024-2025 Student Book and Tools process:

Once you are authorized to purchase books and tools, you have the option to wait an hour before going to the Bookstore to allow the information to be updated, or you may go to the Registrar's office to receive a copy of your schedule to use in the Bookstore Immediately. Students at other campuses will reach out to Mike Mays or Patrick Glynn in the admissions offices to receive their required materials. All General Education classes will have digital books. See Bookstore for any questions.
