My favorite CD's

The Sticks 10/10
       This is my FAVORITE CD by them. a wonderful mixture of indie rock and folk punk. While the whole CD is AMAZING my favorite song has to be To the Wild a song about escaping from society back to a more animalistic primal side of humanity stripping yourself of all of societies burdens.
       One other thing about this CD I adore, is that it feels very close to my personal struggles as a trans women, especially if you listen to the song All Gone which is considered a bonus track in the CD. I won't go into more detail here about it. but god do I relate to it.
You can listen to it Here!
Touch Up 9/10
       Even though this is 2nd on my favorites list it still is just as good as The Sticks While this CD is still considered indie Rock it manages to have a sort of country vibe within it. but enough about how it sounds lets get into what it's about!
       Touch up is all about bodies, specifically the trauma of being treated poorly because of how yours looks. A great example of this is the song Oh Ana a song about starving and self harming ones self; in an attempt to achieve beauty. in a way playing god with ones self...
       you can even witness the singers perception through the repitition of the lyrics. repeatedly stating I'll be God I'll be God, I'll be God, I'll be God today at the start of every verse.
       Like before I could go into more detail, but the song is rather graphic. so if you are in the right mind space for it.
Listen to it Here!
O My Heart 7/10
        My third favorite CD by MOTHER MOTHER. this CD is by far the most INDIE ROCK of all three CD and likewise, one of their most popular CD. Even getting a full studio remaster!
       While a lot of people love the song Hayloft I personally prefer the song Wisdom. A song about regrets, feeling like you wasted your youth on dumb stuff. I could go on and on about why this song is important to me; but that'd require an essay. all you need to know, is that I really have a soft spot for this particular song.
You can listen to it Here!
