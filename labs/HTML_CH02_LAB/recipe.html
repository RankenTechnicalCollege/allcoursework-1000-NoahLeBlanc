French Toast Recipe!

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces of bread
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 Tbsp. sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. ground cinnamon
  • 2 Tbsp. vanilla extract
  • syrup for serving

How to prepare in 10 easy steps!:

  1. Mix together your eggs, butter, sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla
  2. Dunk your bread into the mixture, fully coat them!
  3. set them aside on a plate to use later
  4. heat a nonstick skillet over low heat
  5. then spray some pam(or put a small amount of butter) in the skillet
  6. add as many pieces of bread that can fit, onto the skillet
  7. then increase the heat to just below medium low
  8. allow the toast to cook for 4 minutes then flip and repeat
  9. repeat for the rest of bread
  10. server with syrup! You got this!
