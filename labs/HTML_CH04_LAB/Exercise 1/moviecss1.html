Spider-Man: Enter the Spider-Verse

Spider-Man: Enter the Spider-Verse is an animated movie about Spider-Man.
but not the one you're probably used to, this Spider-Man is Miles Morales.
Not Peter Parker.
the Movie follows this new spider-man, as he meets alternative versions of himself, from different universes.
it's one of my favorite movies due to the amazing animation, the incredible action, and the amazing story.
I highly recommend it!

The spider-people in it

Main Actors

  • Shameik Moore as Miles Morales
  • Jake Johnson as Peter B. Parker

  • John Mulaney as Spider-Ham

Main Actresses
